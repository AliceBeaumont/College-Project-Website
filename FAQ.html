Where are we located?

We are located in the Hillmorton area a few doors down from the train station.

How old do you have to be to benefit from our services?

People of any age can make use of our services, however if you wish to benefit from the HRT portion of our help then you must be 18 years of age, or have a consenting adult present.

Where do you take donations?

In our shop we have a designated drop-off point placed near the front door for people to drop off donations.

Is there a minimum amount to donate?

There is no minimum amount that you have to meet in order to donate, every little helps!

What do my donations do?

They help to fund our charity acquiring the recourses in order to help the youths, none of the donation money goes towards the upkeep of the charity location, this comes directly from our own pockets!
